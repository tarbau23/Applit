Frequently Asked Questions

Is job search really free? +

Yes! Job search and matching is 100% free forever. You only pay for cover letter generation and CV correction services.

How does CV correction work? +

Our CV correction is a one-time service. We professionally format your CV to German standards, correct language errors, and optimize it for ATS systems. Once corrected, you can update and use it unlimited times.

Can I switch plans? +

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. If you have remaining cover letters from a Standard pack, they'll remain valid when you switch.

What languages do you support? +

We support 50+ languages. You can write your CV and cover letters in any language, and we'll translate and format them to professional German standards.

Do unused cover letters expire? +

For the Standard pack (30 letters), your credits are valid for 90 days. Unlimited monthly plans renew each month. Pay-as-you-go letters never expire.
